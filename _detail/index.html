Small Rectangular Box

Gold Leaf / Flowers of the Four Seasons

(Naga-kobako Kinpaku Shiki Soka)

Small Rectangular Box / Gold Leaf / Flowers of the Four Seasons is...

  • This is a "kobako," or small box, with a floral design of 4 seasons.

D rawn by using "maki-e" techniques, each flower represents different seasons: "ume" (plums) in the spring, "botan" (peonies) in the summer, "kiku" (chrysanthemums) in the autumn, and "tessen" (clematis) in the winter.

The gold lid is coated with "hon-kinpaku," or pure gold leaf, to create a luxurious mood.

The velveteen lining inside will protect its contents from being scratched.

Size (cm) : 18.5 * 12 * 7

Material   : Wood, velveteen (lining)

Quality    : Urethane URUSHI (lacquer), KINPAKU (gold leaf)

Note       : Shipping with "kibako" (wooden box)
