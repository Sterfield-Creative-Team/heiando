"Shokunin" Craftsman/ Meister

It is the generic name of a person who has traditional knowledge and skills of their own, and create things by hand.
In Yamada Heiando, generally speaking, person who concerned with each stage of work of create lacquerware (e.g. process wood, paint lacquer, paint "Maki-e", etc. ) is called "Shokunin".
Owing to the techniques that they have developed over many years, lacquerware has been created.
