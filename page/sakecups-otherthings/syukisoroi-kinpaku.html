Shuki-soroi Kinpaku is...

  • This is a set of "sakazuki" cups and a "tokkuri" bottle for drinking Japanese sake.

Each is coated on the surface with "hon-kinpaku," or pure gold leaf, to create a luxurious mood.

These gorgeous vessels are used in Japan to drink sake on special holidays ("hare no hi") or at turning points of one's life. Their sophisticated taste of Japonism fits perfectly to auspicious scenes where people drink toasts.

The sparkling coats of "hon-kinpaku" will last long without coming off, for "URUSHI" lacquer has been applied for protection.

Variation Code 01 is a set of 2 "sakazuki" cups and a "tokkuri" bottle.
Variation Code 02 is a single "sakazuki" cup for an additional purchase.

This bottle and cup with sophisticated and traditional taste of Japan are very suitable to be used in auspicious scene or gorgeous scene.
The “Hon-Kin-paku” that embellished to the whole is hard to come off, because it is coated its surface with lacquer.
