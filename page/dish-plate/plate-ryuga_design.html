"Ryu-ga" (龍牙 Dragon) / large

This square plate is of our signature "Ryu" Series (龍シリーズ), whose name indicates its representation of a dragon flying in the sky. From the ancient times, dragons have been worshiped by the Japanese for their grand powers. Dragons, as embodiments of the light, continue to attract us with the divine beauty that no other creature has.

The design of "Ryu-ga" (龍牙), in particular, portrays a striking rise of dragons with brush strokes in gold. One by one, our exclusive craftsmen hand-paint the strokes with their "maki-e" technique, a traditional Japanese method of gilding. It is their elaborate skills and years of experience that enable them to smoothly sprinkle gold leaf without spreading outside, making a vivid contrast of colours and textures with the black surface.

The plate suits both Japanese and Western cuisine.

* Best as a gift for parents/guardians, housewarming, and Japanese food lovers. *
* Suitable especially for dinner (meat, fish, and pasta). *
