Sun & Moon

(Plate Nichigetsu)

Golden Plate / Sun & Moon is...

  • These are traditional plates for serving Japanese sushi.

They have been coated with "hon-kinpaku," or pure gold leaf, to create a luxurious mood.

Their simple yet beautiful design is called "nichigetsu." The contrast between black and gold naturally draws your eyes and highlights food in the center.

Variation Code 01 is in large size.
Variation Code 02 is in small size.
