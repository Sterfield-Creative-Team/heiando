Chopsticks in Pair

Gold

(Fufu Hashi Kinsai, Kinsen, Gokaku)

Chopsticks in Pair / Gold are...

  • These are traditional chopsticks with gold accents.

Variation Code 01 - 02 are square in shape.
Variation Code 03 is pentagon in shape.

All orders come in a special wooden box, with a pair of each colour (1 red and 1 black). Black is larger than red.
