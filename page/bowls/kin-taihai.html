Tai-hai Kin

Tai-hai Kin is...

  • This is a "sakazuki" cup for drinking Japanese sake, glittering gold in colour and enormous in size.

It is coated with "kinpaku," or gold leaf, to create an impressive mood of luxury.

With the size 5 times larger than usual, this "sakazuki" cup can be used at celebratory scenes where people gather.
