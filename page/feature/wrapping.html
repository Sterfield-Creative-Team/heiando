Gifts, Wrapped with Love.

Original Logo
On Wrapping Paper and Tags.

YAMADA HEIANDO's items are the perfect gifts for every occasion and recipient, with our original wrapping to make your gifts special. Because we want our items to contribute to both givers and recipients to become happy, we will send all items gift wrapped (We do not charge extra for wrapping. It is free.).

As for materials, we use wrapping paper and tags that we have specially prepared. They imprint our original logo of a lidded soup bowl, the traditional symbol of Japanese lacquerware. We have been cherishing this brand logo for more than 90 years since our foundation in Tokyo.

We select just the right size box for each item, so that you can give your gift to your recipient as soon as it arrives.

Just In Case: Handling Manual.

We understand that everyone feels nervous (yet exciting) before starting something new. Don't worry even if it is your first time to use lacquerware - We will send with each item a compact handling manual. Written inside are the fundamental philosophy of YAMADA HEIANDO, the history of lacquerware, and easy steps of how to take care of them in daily life. The descriptions are in English as well as in Japanese.

Gifts for Everyone,
from Japanese Emperor to Your Loved Ones.

Over 90 years of history and heritage, we have always been serving the finest lacquered products. The wide range of items, from classic set of tableware to modern line of stationery, has familiarized YAMADA HEIANDO as the best lacquerware shop in Japan. Our items are often sent as gifts, sometimes in business scenes to your clients and colleagues, other times at intimate occasions to your family and loved ones.

Our customers include the Japanese Imperial Household, who has granted us the "Japanese" Royal Warrant to mark our quality service. Foreign Embassies of Japan, too, have been favouring us and continue to choose our lacquerware to give to foreign diplomats at international meetings. Others are renowned places of worship, such as Grand Shrine of Ise, Kanda Shrine, Naritasan Shinshoji Temple, Furumine Temple, and Manganji Temple; and legal entities of long-standing department stores, global enterprises, and major banks. As for private customers, individuals throughout the world sum up 60% of our overall sales revenue.

We feel more than honoured that we take part in making both givers and recipients happy through the act of gift-giving. Such feeling is shared with our exclusive craftsmen, whose elaborate skills and years of experience have entitled them as governmentally "Certified Traditional Craftsmen." Staying true to craftsmanship and always providing items at their best quality, YAMADA HEIANDO will continue to strive to be your favourite gift shop.
