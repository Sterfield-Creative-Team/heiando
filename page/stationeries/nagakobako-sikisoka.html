Naga-kobako Kinpaku Shiki-soka

Naga-kobako Kinpaku Shiki-soka is...

  • This is a "kobako," or small box, with a floral design of 4 seasons.

Drawn by using "maki-e" techniques, each flower represents different seasons: "ume" (plums) in the spring, "botan" (peonies) in the summer, "kiku" (chrysanthemums) in the autumn, and "tessen" (clematis) in the winter.

The gold lid is coated with "hon-kinpaku," or pure gold leaf, to create a luxurious mood.

The velveteen lining inside will protect its contents from being scratched.
