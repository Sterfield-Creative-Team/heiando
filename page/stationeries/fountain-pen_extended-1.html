Keep it Safe
in Traditional Wooden Box.

We will put each item in a special wooden box upon delivery. This is how the Japanese has been storing works of the highest values, including art pieces at museums.

The box is hand-carved of paulownia trees. It fits more than perfectly with the matching lid, so tight that humidity, heat, and even mould are not likely to affect inside.

Item name is written on the lid in Japanese.
