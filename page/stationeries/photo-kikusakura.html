This is a photo frame depicting the "Sakura" and the "Kiku" with using a traditional technique called "MAKIE".
"Sakura" and "Kiku" depicted on this photo frame is the traditional flower on behalf of Japan.
"Sakura" is a symbol of spring, it has been recognized as celebrate the turning point of life. "Kiku" is a symbol of autumn, it has been loved by the royal court of ancient Japan.
The contrast between jet black and gold is a classical design of Japanese which is inherited from ancient times.
You can use this photo frame in two different ways, horizontal or vertical.

When you want to display a photo of vertically long, please place it vertically.
When you want to display a photo on horizontally long, please place it horizontally.
We have provided two sizes large and small, so you can choose appropriate one, to fit to the size of the photo you want to display.
